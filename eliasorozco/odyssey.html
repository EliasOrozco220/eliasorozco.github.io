The Odyssey Comic

2025

(December 16, 2025)

This was part of my art project for finals. It is a comic adaptation book based off of book 9 from the Odyssey (a translated version). The event was the main character (Odysseus) was stuck in a cyclops' (Polyphemus') cave with his soldiers. I converted it into a short comic book because it was something I wanted to try for a while and I am satisfied with how it came out after a week of doing it. I created all 12 of these pages using Procreate on my iPad.
