This hyperlink is a short story I made up about surviving an apocalypse. It is supposed to be a Sci-fi/Horror themed hyperlink I have done. With the choices you make, you see if you end up surviving certain situations or dying in them. You stumble across these alien creatures that are rapidly taking over the world, eating people and mimic human characteristics in a sloppy way while trying to take over everywhere. The choices you make lead up to what you want to happen to your story and a chance to survive in any way possible. It's very short and doesn't have crazy lore behind it but I enjoyed making it.
