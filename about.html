About

As an artist, i don't have a main focus exactly, but rather i just do what comes to mind. although some works don't correlate with others, they each hold their own meaning and tell their own stories either through looking at it or literally reading what it's about in its description. I am a mexican-american artist based in san jose, california, working mostly with digital media art, 3-d sculpture, and drawing, as i consider these my stronger points as of for now. I do use my culture for one of my works, going back to aztec times and it came out nicely, i aspire to make more works just like it. My works show projects such as glitch work, mashups, story-inspired works, and color themes. I do want to make an art serious sometime but i'm just creating whatever for now. While i'm still working on myself as an artist and how i want to pursue it as a career, i want my work to be inspiring to people in a matter of using your mind the best of your ability and making your thoughts turn into a reality if you really like the idea. Also that there is no limit to what you create, so just go for it. ​​​

Feel free to explore the portfolio and get in touch via the contact page.
